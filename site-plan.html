Sewing Website Site Plan

Brooklyn Sowards

WDD 130-08

Overview

Purpose

The website will provide a place to keep track of my work as I progress in skill, functioning as a portfolio that I can show to others. It wil also be a collection of websites where I have found inspiration. This can act as a resource for others with an interest in sewing and quilting.

Audience

My audience will be potential clients or employers who can view my work, and also friends who are interested in sewing to view my resources and find inspiration.

Content

Home page

This website will allow people to perform activities like: searching using provided links to find their own preferred resources (websites), viewing my work in a portfolio, and contact me for any other questions / requests / suggestions.

Images for the Home page

Portfolio page

This page will be my portfolio of work, if I decide to start a business or just for anyone who is interested in commissioning me to do a project. It will be updated regularly (therefore needs to have an adjustable amount of space for the gallery in the design). This will include quilts, book bags, and any other projects I have tried. There will be a description for each one. I'll want to give information explaining my portfolio (including details like dates and maybe even a rough pattern for each project to explain how I did it).

Images for the Portfolio page

Inspiration page

This page will have information on sewing, quilting, resources such as inspiration ideas, in the form of a central collection of site links (websites that have design ideas or sell products that I like). I'll have a list of my favorite websites for buying fabric, buying other tools/materials, patterns, and other resources. I will have a page or a section of a page where I can display projects and examples that I would like to do/ work on. Maybe a section for projects that are in progress?
